About

I am a software engineer and musician based in London. I enjoy building and making things, which is shown through my programming and music projects.

Largely self-taught as a programmer, I've found I make the most progress when trying out projects and getting hands on with bringing ideas into reality - this is my favourite part of coding.

During my St Andrews masters course I was exposed to varied aspects of computer science, some of which can be seen on the projects page.

My music projects can be found here. I play and write in The Montaines band, enjoy putting together projects with talented musicians, and I'm working on some material of my own.
